Describe ways to improve productivity and efficiency.

To improve productivity you can

  • Track and limit how much time you're spending on tasks
  • Take regular breaks to help with focus
  • Set self-imposed deadlines, this way you have a target to aim for
  • Minimise multitasking on task you have running in the background
