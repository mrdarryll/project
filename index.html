Unit 1.5

Describe how you would go about selecting and using IT systems and software – how do you select the right kind of technology for the job?

The thing about technology is that it includes a wide range of choices to choose from, which means the key is finding the "right" solution for your problems. It's important to figure out which tech will help in the development of your business from those that will eventually end up being simply one more toy to use. What can be done is picking technology that grows with the business, buying new technology is probably going to be costly, so considering what will need to be purchased is a must. Furthermore dissecting your business needs is important. Have an inventory of what technology is used now and how well it is serving the business then you can make an informed decision on next steps. Finally having a backup plan is important. Technology fails. It's an unavoidable truth. On the off chance that this was not anticipated, the possibility of the business closing down completely for a long period could occur. Doing this will assist you in gathering the data you should need in case of technological failures.

GDPR stands for the General Data Protection Regulation, and is the new European privacy regulation that came into effect on May 25, 2018.
